About Me

I was a PhD candidate in the Department of Electrical Engineering at Princeton University. My advisers are Peter Ramadge and Ingrid Daubechies. My interests include statistical signal processing, geometry of learning and manifold learning, harmonic analysis, compressive sensing, and their applications to inverse problems, biomedical imaging, and forgery detection in art.
